The Alcon Project


The Challenge:

The Alcon Project presented unique challenges from the outset. The General Contractor faced the daunting task of renovating and expanding an existing cemical facility in Georgia. The project's complexity lay in the need to precisely understand the as-built conditions of the structure before any work could begin. Traditional surveying methods would be time-consuming and cost-prohibitive for such a sizable and intricate project.

Our Solution:

BIM Technologies stepped in with an innovative solution: Laser Scanning and As-Built Modeling. Our expert team employed state-of-the-art laser scanning technology to capture highly accurate 3D data of the existing structure, inside and out. This data was then meticulously processed and transformed into a comprehensive As-Built Model, providing a digital representation of the current conditions of the building.

The Benefits:

  1. Savings:
    Traditional surveying methods would have taken weeks, if not months, to gather the required data. Our laser scanning and modeling process reduced this time to a fraction, allowing the General Contractor to move forward with the project swiftly.
