About Us

Pioneering BIM Excellence Since 2000

At BIM Technologies, we are trailblazers in the field of Building Information Modeling (BIM). Established in 2000, our journey began with a vision to streamline construction processes and elevate project outcomes.

Our firm specializes in supporting contractors and owners throughout the design-construction-turnover process by providing BIM services at every level. We are MEP and Process Piping Specialist from Design to Fabrication and Install and have over 25 years of experience in these complex systems.

Our Services

Comprehensive BIM Services Tailored to Your Needs

Virtual and Augmented Reality

Our team specializes in animations, realistic materiality, and physics based lighting.

Laser Scanning

With top of the line equipment, our technicians can quickly mobilize to scan any site and produce a field verified digital clone.

Construction Administration

Details are our specialty. Let us manage your construction projects and ensure effeciancy communicating with all trades.

Coordination

With our strengths in autodesk software, including Revit and Navisworks, we offer a comprehensive clash detection solution in design or construction for all trades.

Data Management

We work with your team to develop a streamlined and consistent strategy to consolidate all design data and eliminate disconnected or paper-based processes.

3D Modeling

Our 3D models bring your design concepts to life, allowing you to explore and present your vision with unparalleled clarity.

As-Built Modeling

As-Built models serve as an accurate reflection of the real-world conditions, dimensions, and components of the structure. Our provided models are extremely valuable for documentation, facility management, renovation planning, and assessing the accuracy of construction work.
